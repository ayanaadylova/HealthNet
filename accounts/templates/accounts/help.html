{% if request.user.patient %}

How to Create an Appointment:

  • Navigate to your Dashboard by clicking the Dashboard button in the navbar on the top of the page
  • If you have yet to enter in important Medical Info, there will be a button requesting for you to fill out this info at the bottom of your calendar before making an appointment. Please do so as you will be unable to create an Appointment before you do.
  • If you have already entered in the relevant medical info, you will see a button on the bottom of your calendar labeled 'Make Appointment', Click on this button.
  • After clicking on the button, enter in a name for the Appointment, and the day you wish to have the appointment on.
  • After Entering in the date and progressing to the next part of the process, enter in the desired start time of your appointment. (Note: you will only be able to create a 30 minute long appointment, a longer appointment must be scheduled by a doctor or nurse)
  • Click Submit to create the appointment, you can view the appointment on you calendar, and click on it to update details, or delete it. Your doctor will automatically be notified of the appointment.

How to View My Profile:

  • From the Navigation bar on the top of your screen, click on the 'Profile' button, this will bring you to a view of your profile.

How to Change My Profile Info:

  • From the View of your Profile, click on the 'Update Profile Info' button on the bottom right of the panel.

How to Change My Password:

  • From the View of your Profile, click the 'Change Password' button on th bottom right of the panel.
  • Enter in the new password you wish to have to log in to your account.
  • Re-Enter the new password in the second field.
  • Click submit to change your password, you will then be able to Log in with your new password. (Note: Your previous password will no longer work to log you in, so it is recommended that you pick a new password that you will remember.)

How to View My Medical Info:

  • From you Navigation Bar at the top of your screen, click the 'EMR' buttom, EMR stands for 'Electronic Medical Record'

How to Print My Medical Info:

  • From the view of your Electronic Medical Record, click on the button on the top right of the first panel on the screen, the button has a small picture of a printer on it.
  • Select the printer you wish to print to and click the 'Print' button, then check your printer.
{% elif request.user.doctor %}

How to create an Appointment for a Patient

  • Navigate to your Dashboard by clicking on the Dashboard button in the Navigation bar at the top of your screen.
  • Click on the button labeled 'Make Appointment' below your calendar.
  • Enter in a name for the appointment, select the patient and select a date. Then click Submit
  • Select a beginning and an end time for the appointment from the drop down menu.
  • Click Submit and the new appointment should appear on your calendar, click on it to edit details of the appointment or delete it.

How to set aside Personal Time on your schedule

  • Navigate to your Dashboard by clicking on the Dashboard button in the Navigation menu at the top of your screen.
  • Click on the button labeled 'Make Appointment'
  • Enter in a name for the time (Ex: 'Racquetball')
  • Leave the Patient field Blank.
  • Select a date form the drop down, then hit Submit.
  • The new Personal time slot will appear on your calendar, and Patients and Nurses will be unable to create new Appointments for you during this time slot.

How to add a Prescription to a Patient:

  • Navigate to your Patient list from the 'Patient List' button on the navigation bar at the top of your screen.
  • Click on the EMR for the Patient for whom the Prescription will be made out to.
  • Click on the 'Add Prescription' button on the second panel of the Patient's Electronic Medical Record.
  • Enter in the name of the drug being Prescribed in the first field.
  • Enter in Information about the Prescription in the second field.
  • Enter in a start and end date for the Prescription and refills.
  • Enter in the dosage number, and the dosage units in the bottom 2 fields.
  • Click submit to create the Prescription which should now appear on the Patient's EMR.

How to add a Test Result to a Patient:

  • Navigate to your Patient list from the 'Patient List' button on the navigation bar at the top of your screen.
  • Click on the EMR for the Patient for whom the Test Result is for.
  • Click on the 'Add Test Result' button on the third panel on the EMR.
  • Enter a name for the Test Result and a date.
  • Enter in the relevant text information for the Test result.
  • Click on the Choose file button to add a picture or other file to the Test Result.
  • Select whether the patient will see the results of this test in the checkbox.
  • Click Submit and the Test result will be added to the Patient's EMR.

How to update a Patient's Medical Info:

  • Navigate to your Patient list from the 'Patient List' button on the navigation bar at the top of your screen.
  • Click on the EMR for the Patient for whom you wish to change the medical info.
  • Click on the 'Update Medical Info' button on the first panel of the EMR.
  • Enter in relevant information into the fields and click submit.
  • The new information will now be displayed on the Patient's EMR.

How to Send a Message to a Staff Member:

  • Click on the Messages button from the navigation menu at the top of your screen.
  • Click Send message to send a new message.
  • Select a Recipient from the Users menu, use the Search bar if there are a lot of Entries.
  • After selecting a User, select the priority of the message and type in the subject.
  • Type in the contents of the message in the Message field.
  • Click submit after you finish typing the message.

How to View Messages Sent to You:

  • Click on the Messages button from the navigation menu at the top of your screen.
  • Click on the Inbox tab below the 'Send Message' button.
  • Click on the message you would like to view, they are listed by their priority set by the sender.

How to Admit a Patient:

  • Navigate to the Dashboard using the Navigation Bar at the top of the screen.
  • Just below the Navigation Bar there is a group of buttons, click on the button that says 'Admit Patient'.
  • Select the Patient you wish to admit.
  • Select the Hospital from the Hospitals you work at that you wish to admit the Patient to.
  • Select a reason from the drop down menu and any relevant details into the details field.
  • Click submit and the Patient will be admitted into the hospital within the system.

How to Discharge a Patient:

  • Navigate to the Dashboard using the Navigation Bar at the top of the screen.
  • Just below the navigation bar there is a group of buttons, click on the button labeled 'Discharge a Patient'.
  • Select the Patient you wish to discharge.
  • Enter in a reason for discharging the patient.
  • Click submit to discharge the patient from the system.

How to Transfer a Patient;

  • Navigate to the Dashboard using the Navigation Bar at the top of the screen.
  • Just below the navigation bar there is a group of buttons, click on the button labeled 'Discharge a Patient'.
  • Select the patient you wish to transfer.
  • Select the hospital you wish to transfer the patient to.
  • Select the new doctor for the patient.
  • click submit and the patient will be marked as in transit to the new hospital until the new doctor accepts the patient on the other side.

How to View my Profile Info:

  • From the Navigation bar on the top of your screen, click on the 'Profile' button, this will bring you to a view of your profile.

How to Change my Profile Info:

  • From the View of your Profile, click on the 'Update Profile Info' button on the bottom right of the panel.

How to Change my Password:

  • From the View of you Profile, click on the 'Change Password' button on the botton right of the panel.
  • Enter in the new password in the first field, and re-enter the new password in the second.
  • Click submit and your password will be changed.
